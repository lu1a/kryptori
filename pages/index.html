Kryptori

About

Like Ebay but for crypto etc.
Not an escrow service, just getting two parties in touch so that they can exchange virtual and physical goods.
The idea is that you and the other party conduct your affairs over email, because that encourages your liberty as opposed to trying to keep your data in some proprietary in-app chat.

...what, you don't like the UI?! Make a pull request to the source code then: github.com/lu1a/kryptori.
